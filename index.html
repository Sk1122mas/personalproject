Reduced Air Quality

In Malaysia, The air quality have been heavily impacted. Mostly, the sources of the pollution comes from our use of transportation. Since Malaysian citizens often relies on using cars to get around their area, it often causes massive traffic and increase of the usage of cars. The exhaust pipe from the multiple cars is the main cause of the worsen air quality. The worsen air quality would also impact the human health. For example in Johor, the concentration of pollutants is PM2.5 as of September 9, 2024. Unfiiting for human health as it is 3.6 times worse than the WHO guideline value. It also reduce the quality of life, as many people who lived in Malaysia or born in Malaysia have stated that there is an unpleasant smell in the air. This poses a threat to people who have respiratory health problems.

Environment In Danger

Malaysia, ranking 12th globally in its biodiversity, is now facing a devastating situation as the severe pollution in the country threatens to endanger the environment. For example, toxic wastes dumped into the rivers have affected the marine wildlives and killed many fishes, also reducing the amount of fishes that fishermen will fish for. Right now, the clean water that Malaysia used to have and has become dirty and polluted, almost unsightly and embarrassing to see as a Malaysian. Pollution is also the cause of habitat loss in Malaysia.

Action you should take

In order to save Malaysia from pollutants, we have to take action. One small step could lead to big changes.

Use more public transport

People should be able to take note to use more public transportation rather than driving their own car. This way, the pollutant emitted into the air will be signficiantly reduced.

Sort and throw your trash properly

Sorting out trash into different catergories (such as tin, papers, plasic) to be seperated. This is so that that the trash can be easily sorted out by the trash manager to determine which can be recycled or which can be used for biodegration.

Recycle whenever possible

Recycling is important, especially that it is one of the method to reduce waste so that it will also help reduce pollution. Recycling can also help you save money and create new things too.
